05 Days Spiritual Circuit

The Sacred Heart of India

Experience the "Anant Kashi Ayodhya Yatra with Prayagraj." This journey connects the three most sacred points in Uttar Pradesh. From the liberation of Kashi and the royalty of Ayodhya to the holy confluence of three rivers at Prayagraj, this is the ultimate spiritual pilgrimage.


TOUR HIGHLIGHTS
  • Varanasi: Kashi Vishwanath Darshan and Evening Ganga Aarti.
  • Prayagraj: Holy dip at Triveni Sangam (Confluence of Ganga, Yamuna & Saraswati).
  • Ayodhya: Visit the historic Shri Ram Janmabhoomi Temple and Hanumangarhi.
  • Sarnath: Excursion to the place where Lord Buddha gave his first sermon.
  • Inclusions: AC Transfers, Premium Stays, and Guided Darshans.

5 Days Sacred Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Varanasi & Ganga Aarti

Arrive at Varanasi. Evening witness the spiritual Ganga Aarti. Overnight in Varanasi.

Day 2: Varanasi - Prayagraj - Varanasi

Early morning drive to **Prayagraj**. Take a holy dip at the **Triveni Sangam**. Visit Anand Bhawan and Hanuman Temple. Return to Varanasi for overnight stay.

Day 3: Varanasi to Ayodhya

Visit Kashi Vishwanath Temple and Sarnath. Afternoon drive to the holy city of Ayodhya. Overnight in Ayodhya.

Day 4: Full Day Ayodhya Darshan

Full day visit to Shri Ram Janmabhoomi, Kanak Bhawan, and Hanumangarhi. Evening Sarayu River visit. Overnight in Ayodhya.

Day 5: Departure via Lucknow

After breakfast, check out and drive to Lucknow airport for your return flight home.
