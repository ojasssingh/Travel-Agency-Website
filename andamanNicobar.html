06 Days Tour

Andaman Fully Loaded With Neil Stay

Experience the ultimate Andaman getaway with this all-inclusive 5 Night / 6 Day "Fully Loaded" package. This tour is curated to include all the "must-see" sightseeing spots including Port Blair, Havelock Island (Swaraj Dweep), and Neil Island (Shaheed Dweep).


TOUR HIGHLIGHTS
  • Port Blair (2N): Guided tour of Cellular Jail with Sound & Light show.
  • Havelock Island (2N): Radhanagar Beach Sunset with Family Photo Session.
  • Neil Island (1N): Visit Bharatpur, Laxmanpur Beach & Natural Howrah Bridge.
  • Adventure: Self-Drive Sea Kart session at Corbyn's Cove Beach.
  • Transfers: Inter-island travel via Makruzz/Coastal Cruise/Green Ocean.

6 Days Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Port Blair - Cellular Jail & Light Show

Arrive at Port Blair. Visit the Historic Cellular Jail followed by the evening Light & Sound show. Overnight at Port Blair.

Day 2: Port Blair to Havelock Island

Travel by luxury cruise to Havelock. Visit Radhanagar Beach (Asia's #1 beach) for a stunning sunset and a complimentary family photo session.

Day 3: Elephant Beach Excursion

Enjoy a boat ride to Elephant Beach. Indulge in snorkeling or just relax on the pristine white sands. Overnight at Havelock.

Day 4: Havelock to Neil Island

Cruise to Neil Island. Visit Bharatpur Beach and the Natural Bridge (subject to tide conditions). Overnight at Neil Island.

Day 5: Neil Island to Port Blair

Visit Laxmanpur Beach. Return to Port Blair via cruise. Evening free for shopping at local markets.

Day 6: Departure from Port Blair

After breakfast, transfer to Veer Savarkar Airport for your return flight home.
