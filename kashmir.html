Exotic Kashmir With Gulmarg Stay

Discover "Heaven on Earth" with our premium 6 Night / 7 Day curated journey. This tour is designed to showcase the very best of the valley, featuring a romantic houseboat stay in Srinagar, the lush meadows of Pahalgam, and an overnight stay in the winter wonderland of Gulmarg.


TOUR HIGHLIGHTS
  • Srinagar (3N): Includes 1 Night in a luxury Houseboat & Shikara ride on Dal Lake.
  • Pahalgam (2N): Explore Betaab Valley, Aru Valley, and the Lidder River.
  • Gulmarg (1N): Experience the world-famous Gondola Ride (Phase 1 included).
  • Heritage: Visit Mughal Gardens (Nishat & Shalimar) and Martand Sun Temple ruins.
  • Unique Experiences: Saffron cultivation demonstration at Pampore and a visit to a Cricket Bat Factory.

7 Days Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Srinagar - Houseboat & Shikara

Arrive at Srinagar airport. Transfer to a traditional Houseboat on Dal Lake. Enjoy a romantic 1-hour Shikara ride during sunset. Overnight in Houseboat.

Day 2: Srinagar to Pahalgam (Valley of Shepherds)

Drive to Pahalgam. Enroute visit Saffron fields at Pampore and the 9th-century Avantipur ruins. Relax by the Lidder River. Overnight in Pahalgam.

Day 3: Pahalgam Sightseeing

Full day to explore Aru Valley, Betaab Valley, and Chandanwari (starting point of Amarnath Yatra). Optional pony ride to Baisaran Meadow.

Day 4: Pahalgam to Gulmarg (Meadow of Flowers)

Drive to Gulmarg. Enroute visit the ancient Martand Sun Temple and a Kashmiri Cricket Bat Factory. Evening at leisure in the meadows. Overnight in Gulmarg.

Day 5: Gulmarg Gondola & Return to Srinagar

Experience the Gulmarg Gondola (Asia's highest cable car). Proceed to Phase 1 (Khilanmarg) for snow views. Afternoon drive back to Srinagar. Overnight in Srinagar Hotel.

Day 6: Sonmarg Excursion (Meadow of Gold)

Full-day excursion to Sonmarg. Visit the Thajiwas Glacier (via pony or sledge). Return to Srinagar for evening shopping at Residency Road. Overnight in Srinagar.

Day 7: Departure from Srinagar

Morning visit to Shankaracharya Temple and Mughal Gardens. Later, transfer to Srinagar airport for your flight home.
