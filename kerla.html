06 Days / 05 Nights

Experience God’s Own Country

Explore the best of Kerala with our "Kerala Delight" package. This tour takes you from the colonial charm of Kochi to the mist-covered tea gardens of Munnar, the spice-scented air of Thekkady, and the tranquil backwaters of Alleppey.


TOUR HIGHLIGHTS
  • Kochi: Experience India's first Water Metro ride and visit Fort Kochi.
  • Munnar (2N): Visit Eravikulam National Park and lush Tea Estates.
  • Thekkady (1N): Spice plantation tour, Elephant ride, and Kalaripayattu show.
  • Alleppey (1N): Serene boat ride on the world-famous backwaters.
  • Cultural Bliss: Authentic Kerala Sadya lunch included enroute.

6 Days Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Kochi

Arrival at Kochi airport. Transfer to hotel, check-in, and relax. Explore local markets for souvenirs in the evening.

Day 2: Kochi Sightseeing to Munnar

Visit Mattancherry Palace, Jewish Synagogue, and Chinese Fishing Nets. Experience the Water Metro. Drive to Munnar via Cheeyappara Waterfalls.

Day 3: Munnar Full Day Tour

Explore Eravikulam National Park (home to Nilgiri Tahr), Mattupetty Dam, Echo Point, and the Tea Museum.

Day 4: Munnar to Thekkady

Drive to Thekkady. Visit a Spice Plantation, enjoy an Elephant ride, and witness the Kalaripayattu Martial Art show.

Day 5: Thekkady to Alleppey

Drive to Alleppey, the "Venice of the East." Enjoy a relaxing boat ride through the backwater canals. Overnight at Alleppey.

Day 6: Departure from Kochi

After breakfast, transfer back to Kochi Airport for your onward journey with beautiful memories.
