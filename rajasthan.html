Cultural Tour Of Rajasthan

Immerse yourself in the "Land of Maharajas" with our comprehensive 6 Night / 7 Day cultural odyssey. This package covers the famous "Pink City" of Jaipur, the "Blue City" of Jodhpur, and the "City of Lakes," Udaipur. Experience the architectural grandeur of forts, palaces, and heritage temples.


TOUR HIGHLIGHTS
  • Jaipur (2N): Amer Fort, City Palace, and Jantar Mantar (UNESCO site).
  • Jodhpur (2N): Massive Mehrangarh Fort and Jaswant Thada memorial.
  • Udaipur (2N): Romantic City Palace complex and Saheliyon Ki Bari.
  • Enroute Visits: Scenic drives across the Aravalli range.
  • Stay: Handpicked heritage-style or modern comfortable hotels.

7 Days Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Jaipur

Arrive in the "Pink City." Check into your hotel. Evening at leisure to explore the vibrant local bazaars like Johari and Bapu Bazar. Overnight in Jaipur.

Day 2: Jaipur Sightseeing

Visit the Amer Fort (optional Elephant/Jeep ride). Later, explore the City Palace, Hawa Mahal (Photo stop), and the Jantar Mantar observatory.

Day 3: Jaipur to Jodhpur (325 Kms / 7 Hrs)

Drive to Jodhpur, the "Blue City." Upon arrival, check-in. Evening free to explore the antique markets near the Clock Tower.

Day 4: Jodhpur Sightseeing

Explore the Mehrangarh Fort, one of India's largest forts. Visit Jaswant Thada, the marble cenotaph often called the 'Taj Mahal of Marwar.'

Day 5: Jodhpur to Udaipur (260 Kms / 6 Hrs)

Drive to the romantic city of Udaipur. Check into your hotel. Enjoy an evening walk by the banks of Lake Pichola.

Day 6: Udaipur Local Sightseeing

Visit the City Palace, Jagdish Temple, and Saheliyon Ki Bari (Garden of Maidens). Optional boat ride on Lake Pichola.

Day 7: Departure from Udaipur

After breakfast, transfer to Udaipur Airport or Railway Station for your onward journey.
